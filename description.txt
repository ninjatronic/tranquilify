Do you struggle to focus in a noisy office? Do you have trouble going to sleep, or relaxing in stressful situations? Do you need help staying calm?

Bring some peace and tranquility into your life with Tranquilify.

Tranquilify plays you the soothing sounds of nature while you get on with other things, or just relax. Each soundscape is accompanied by a beautiful and calming image, and sounds can be mixed together to create soundscapes of your own. They will even play in the background while you use other apps.

Tranquilify will help you block distractions from your mind, reduce your stress levels, sooth your headache and mask tinnitus.

The sounds are also accompanied by a selection of peaceful and inspirational quotes.

No wrestling with tons of unnecessary features, no pressure to share or rate, just relax with the sounds of nature.
